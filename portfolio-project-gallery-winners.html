Find The Piece That Tells Your Story

Winners is a chain of off-price Canadian department stores offering brand name clothing, footwear, bedding, furniture, fine jewellery, beauty products, and housewares.
The ask was to generate an increase in sales, while targeting a wider demographic and alter the notion that young adults feel like Winners is a bit old and not for them. Focusing on their #WinnersFabFinds hashtag, unique styles and great prices, with the inclusion of an in-store promotion, we asked consumers to tell us their story.
